What exactly is Pink Unicorn?

Pink Unicorn is MUZVI's loose raspberry leaf ritual. The product format is an herbal infusion, not a gummy or candy.

How much is in each pouch?

Each pouch holds 2.12 oz / 60 g of loose raspberry leaf — about 30 rituals based on a 2 g serving.

How do I prepare it?

Measure 2 g, add about 8 fl oz / 240 ml of hot water, steep for the time printed on your label, then strain.

Does MUZVI claim to treat period pain?

No. MUZVI is a herbal infusion ritual and does not make treatment claims. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease.
